About


The baking journey begins with it and the cafe will intrigue you through an experience of the artisanal baking journey with a bevy of French classics and creative bakes. With the cosy space outfitted in industrial-like grey interiors, rustic wooden furniture and neon orange lighting, along with a show kitchen to watch fresh bakes being baked on the spot, this minimalist cafe lets you find your inner calm away from the hustle in a central yet off-beat Jalan Besar enclave. Perfect for cafe-hoppers, dates, social gatherings and family friendly crowds with its provision of cool coloring sets as you enjoy the comforting brunch fare, delish baked treats and specialty coffee.
